Why is Encryption Important?

Encryption protects data from hackers and unauthorized access by scrambling information. It ensures that only the intended recipient can read it, safeguarding sensitive details like passwords and bank information.

Can Encryption Be Misused?

Yes, encryption can be used for illegal activities, such as hiding cybercrimes. While it ensures privacy, it also presents challenges for law enforcement in tracking harmful activities.
